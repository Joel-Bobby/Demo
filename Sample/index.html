Our Story

Founded on a Clear Mission

CryptoVault was established in 2017 when our founding team — experienced in traditional finance and emerging blockchain technology — recognized that retail investors were navigating one of the most complex and volatile asset classes in history without adequate information resources.

We set out to build an independent intelligence platform free from conflicts of interest: we do not execute trades, we do not hold client assets, and we do not have commercial relationships with the projects we analyze.

Our singular focus is producing the most accurate, objective, and useful research possible and making it accessible to a global community of thoughtful investors.
